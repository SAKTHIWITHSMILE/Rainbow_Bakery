About Rainbow

Three decades ago, a small sweet shop was started by Late K.L. Narayana Moorthi, in the metropolis of Chennai.
This same shop has now flowered into multiple branches that dot the length and breadth of Chennai as well as its neighbouring cities.

M/s. Rainbow Sweets & Snacks is now a well-established brand in Southern India that continues to grow with a
vision to capture the global market. The baton of this sweet legacy was passed on by K.L. Narayana Moorthi to his sons, Mr. K.L.
Venkatesan and Mr. K.L. Srinivasa Moorthi who now act as the driving force behind Rainbow.
